# December 5th, 2025 - Cloudflare APIs and Dashboard experiencing issues

|                          |                  |
| -----------------------: |:-----------------|
|    **Reported on Slack** | December-05-2025 |
|   **Solution Completed** | December-05-2025 |

## What happened?

Cloudflare detected issues on their APIs and their Dashboards. Multiple services across the internet experienced disconnections and downtimes. Decentraland was inaccessible for a period of time due to this incident.

## Why did it happen?

This incident was external to Decentraland and fully caused by cloudflare, more details about the incident are reported here https://www.cloudflarestatus.com/incidents/lfrm31y6sw9q

## Issue Timeline - UTC

1. 2025-12-05 09:00:00 - Foundation members were not able to connect to Decentraland multiplayer services. Initial investigation started.
2. 2025-12-05 09:10:00 - The issue with Cloudflare was reported internally
3. 2025-12-05 09:15:00 - Incident reported (DCL-2113)
4. 2025-12-05 10:15:00 - Cloudflare reported that the issue was being mitigated
5. 2025-12-05 10:30:00 - Cloudflare reported that the issue was fixed
5. 2025-12-05 10:35:00 - Foundation members started being able to connect successfully
5. 2025-12-05 10:40:00 - Incident resolved

## Impact

- **Severity**: SEV-1
- Multiplayer services for Decentraland were offline during the incident.
- Users could reach the login screen on the client but couldn't connect

## Solution

Cloudflare resolved the issue on their end. No action was required from Decentraland's side.
